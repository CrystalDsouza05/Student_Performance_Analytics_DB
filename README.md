## ğŸ“„ Dashboard Structure

### Page 1: Overall Performance
**Purpose:**
To provide a high-level snapshot of student and teacher performance across subjects, time, and demographics.

ğŸ” **Key Visuals**
- **Top Students (Bar Chart):** Highlights the 10 highest scorers for quick recognition.
- **Monthly Trend (Column Chart):** Shows steady improvement in average scores over time.
- **Summary Stats (KPIs):** Displays total students, teachers, subjects, and average score for a quick overview.
- **Top Subjects & Teachers (Table):** Links subject performance to instructor impact.
- **Assessment Outcomes (Pie Chart):** Compares total assessments taken versus passed.
- **Gender Performance (Donut Chart):** Displays weighted scores categorized by gender.

ğŸ§­ **Navigation & Filters**
Includes sidebar links to student and teacher views, plus dropdown filters for **assessment type** and **category**, ensuring smooth navigation and targeted insights.

### Page 2: Student Performance
**Purpose:**
To provide a detailed, student-level view of academic progress, assessment history, and subject-wise performance.

ğŸ” **Key Visuals**
- **Student Profile Card (Flip Card):** Displays personal details such as name, age, nationality, and grade level on the front. The back reveals the studentâ€™s performance overview, offering an engaging and compact insight experience.
- **Assessment Distribution (Donut Chart):** Breaks down 1,268 assessments by subject type to show academic focus.
- **Academic Timeline (Line Chart):** Tracks monthly performance trends to highlight areas of growth or decline.
- **Assessment Summary (Table):** Lists individual assessments with grades, subjects, and teacher names for transparency and comparison.

### Page 3: Teacher Performance
**Purpose:**
To evaluate individual teacher effectiveness through performance trends, assessment data, and comparative metrics.

ğŸ” **Key Visuals**
- **Teacher Profile Card (Flip Card):** Displays teacher name, subject, experience, and department. The reverse side reveals their average scores, contribution to assessments, and other key performance metrics.
- **Performance Trend (Line Chart):** Tracks monthly weighted scores to illustrate consistency and growth.
- **Summary Stats (KPIs):** Highlights total teachers, subjects handled, and average scores (overall vs. individual).
- **Assessment Summary (Table):** Lists assessments conducted, corresponding scores, and categories, reflecting teaching impact and subject performance.

ğŸ§­ **Navigation & Filters**
Includes sidebar links to student and overall views, along with filters for **term**, **academic year**, and **subject type**, allowing multi-level comparative analysis.
